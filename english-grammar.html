English Grammar Basics

30 May 2025

English grammar ko samajhna writing aur speaking dono ke liye zaroori hai. Yahaan kuch basic concepts detail mein samjhaye gaye hain:

1. Tenses

Tenses batate hain ke kaam kab hua:

  • Present Tense – Ab ho raha hai (e.g., I eat, She is reading)
  • Past Tense – Pehle ho chuka (e.g., I ate, She was reading)
  • Future Tense – Aane wale waqt mein hoga (e.g., I will eat, She will be reading)

2. Parts of Speech

Har word ka ek grammatical role hota hai:

  • Noun – Naam (e.g., boy, city)
  • Verb – Kaam (e.g., run, write)
  • Adjective – Quality (e.g., big, smart)
  • Adverb – Verb ko modify karta hai (e.g., quickly, silently)
  • Preposition – Position batata hai (e.g., on, in, under)
  • Conjunction – Words ko jodta hai (e.g., and, but, because)
  • Interjection – Sudden expression (e.g., wow!, oh!)

3. Sentence Structure

A correct sentence mein subject, verb aur object hotay hain. Example:

  • Simple Sentence: She sings.
  • Compound Sentence: She sings and he dances.
  • Complex Sentence: She sings because she is happy.
