Top 5 Math Study Tips

2 June 2025

Math ko asaan banane ke kuch zabardast tips:

  • Practice rozana: Har din kam az kam 30 min math practice karein.
  • Formulas yaad rakhein: Algebra, Geometry aur Trigonometry ke formulas memorize karein.
  • Examples solve karein: Sirf theory mat parhein — examples se samajh aata hai.
  • Concept pe focus karein: Har step samajhna zaroori hai, ratta nahi lagana.
  • Revision karein: Weekly ya monthly revision se concepts fresh rehte hain.
