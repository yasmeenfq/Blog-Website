History of Computers

28 May 2025

Computers ka safar interesting hai — 1800s se le kar AI tak:

  • Charles Babbage: “Father of Computer” — Analytical Engine ka idea diya.
  • ENIAC (1945): Pehla electronic general-purpose computer.
  • 1960s–80s: Mainframes se personal computers tak ka transition.
  • Modern Era: Laptops, smartphones, and cloud computing.
  • Future: Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing aur Robotics!

Computer science duniya ko har roz nayi soorat mein badal raha hai.
