Newton’s Laws Simplified

1 June 2025

Physics ka dar khatam! Newton ke motion ke 3 qanoon simple lafzon mein:

  • First Law: Har object rest ya motion mein rehta hai jab tak external force na lage (Law of Inertia).
  • Second Law: Force = Mass × Acceleration (F = ma), yaani jitni zyada force, utna zyada motion.
  • Third Law: Har action ka equal aur opposite reaction hota hai (Reaction Force).

In laws ka istemal har jagah hota hai — car brakes, sports, rockets, sab mein!
